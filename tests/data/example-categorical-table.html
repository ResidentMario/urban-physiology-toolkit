Statistics form a key factor in the formulation of better policies for the State of Qatar within the framework of the Qatar National Vision 2030 and the national development strategy of 2016/2011. The statistics are important to serve the main pillars of the Qatar National Vision 2030 four and sustainable development of the State of Qatar.
